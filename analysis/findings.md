## Finding 1: Financial constraints are the largest barrier

66.7% of respondents identified financial issues as a major obstacle to educational opportunities.

## Implication:
Students may have the ability and motivation to succeed but lack resources to pursue opportunities.

## Finding 2: Students are motivated but often do not apply

61.5% reported seeing opportunities but not applying.

50% reported sometimes deciding not to apply because they felt unqualified.

## Implication:
The challenge is not motivation alone but confidence and application support.

## Finding 3: Opportunity discovery is fragmented

Students primarily learn about opportunities through:

Social Media (56.4%)
Internet Search (53.8%)
Friends (43.6%)

Teachers accounted for only 25.6%.

## Implication:
There is no centralized system connecting students to opportunities.

## Finding 4: Mentorship remains limited

Only 28.2% reported regular mentorship.

## Implication:
Many students lack guidance when navigating scholarships, internships, and competitions.
